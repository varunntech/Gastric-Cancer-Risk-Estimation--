About Us

Welcome to GastricCare, an advanced AI-powered platform designed to help individuals assess their risk of gastric cancer through a comprehensive, user-friendly interface.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals with accessible, accurate health risk assessments using cutting-edge machine learning technology. We believe that early awareness and risk assessment can lead to better health outcomes.

How It Works

Our platform uses a Random Forest machine learning model trained on comprehensive health data to analyze various risk factors including age, family history, lifestyle choices, and existing medical conditions. The model provides personalized risk assessments categorized as Low, Moderate, or High risk.

Important Disclaimer

This tool is for informational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals for medical decisions. The predictions are based on statistical modeling and should support, not replace, clinical judgment.

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. All data is processed securely, and we do not store personal health information beyond what is necessary for the risk assessment calculation.
